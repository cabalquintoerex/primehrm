Project Name : LGU PRIME-HRM
Project Description : The Civil Service Commission’s Program to Institutionalize Meritocracy and Excellence in Human Resource Management (PRIME-HRM) elevates public sector HR by Assessing, Assisting, and Awarding agencies on four core pillars: Recruitment, Selection, and Placement; Learning and Development; Performance Management; and Rewards and Recognition. It aims to shift HR from transactional to strategic, improving employee experience for better public service. 
Key Aspects of PRIME-HRM
Objectives: The program promotes meritocracy, rewards excellence, and empowers government agencies to improve HR competencies, systems, and practices.
